Purpose

If we think further, even possible psychological states can be observed. In addition, people who have free time at home can enjoy activities, entertainment, education, sports, mini-party, mini-concert, etc. they can search. Thus, we can encourage people to communicate in order to get rid of these situations in the easiest and simplest way, thanks to the applications we will make to meet their activity search. For example, we can enable him to go to an event face-to-face and meet with other people at the specified place on the specified day and time, by choosing an event by considering the situations such as venue, event and information.
